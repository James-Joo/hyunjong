Technological Innovation: Operation & Management

I served as a consultant at the Asian Development Bank (ADB), an international financial organization. The project's objectives were to improve the capacity of 13 Cambodian cities to operate the new sewerage, solid waste, and drainage assets being procured under the ADB projects. The project collaborates with the Cambodian government, such as the Ministry of Public Works and Transport (MPWT) and the Ministry of Economic and Finance (MEF).

Hyundai ZER01NE Fund #2

Secondary PEF: Toshiba Memory Corporation M&A

In June 2018, I completed a secondary underwriting deal to Toshiba's flash memory division's mega USD18 billion sales to a multinational consortium led by US private equity firm Bain Capital.
⨳ My team in Hyundai PE became responsible for reselling Bain's share in the non-convertible preferred stock offering. In addition, it has formed a fund on the scale of KRW560 billion (USD501 million) to acquire the shares along with several Korean institutional players.
